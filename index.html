About Us

Welcome to Shiva Plastics, where innovation meets reliability in the realm of PVC piping solutions. Established in 2007, Shiva Plastics has stood as a beacon of excellence, navigating the currents of industry with steadfast determination and unwavering commitment to quality. At Shiva Plastics, we pride ourselves on being pioneers in the art of PVC pipe manufacturing. From the pristine clarity of ISI-certified pipes to the eco-conscious versatility of recycled pipes, our extensive range spans the spectrum of customer needs. We don't just meet standards; we redefine them. Under the visionary leadership of Sathish G., Shiva Plastics has become synonymous with customer-centricity. Our ethos revolves around one simple principle: customer satisfaction above all else. Every pipe, every fitting, every product that leaves our facility is a testament to our dedication to serving our customers' needs with unparalleled excellence.

But our commitment doesn't stop at pipes alone. We're your one-stop destination for a myriad of PVC solutions. From CPVC pipes to UPVC fittings, from hoses to tanks, and even extending to electrical essentials like switches and bulbs, Shiva Plastics is your trusted partner in every project, big or small. What sets us apart is not just what we offer, but how we deliver it. We believe in going the extra mile to ensure that every customer requirement is not just met, but exceeded. Our relentless pursuit of customer satisfaction drives us to offer competitive pricing without compromising on quality, ensuring that every investment made with Shiva Plastics is an investment in peace of mind. At Shiva Plastics, we don't just build pipes; we build relationships. We understand that behind every project, there's a vision, a dream waiting to be realized. And we consider it our privilege to be a part of that journey, providing the sturdy foundation upon which dreams are built. Join us at Shiva Plastics, where every pipe tells a story of innovation, every fitting speaks of reliability, and every customer interaction is a testament to our unwavering commitment to excellence. Welcome to the Shiva Plastics family, where quality flows through every pipe, and satisfaction is the measure of our success.
